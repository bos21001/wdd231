NP Membership Benefits

The Non-Profit (NP) Membership is designed for organizations that focus on community service and not-for-profit activities. By joining at this level, you will receive:

  • Discounted event participation fees
  • Access to networking opportunities with local businesses
  • Inclusion in our member directory
  • Priority registration for volunteer and sponsorship opportunities

Make a difference while connecting with businesses that share your values!

Bronze Membership Benefits

The Bronze Membership offers small businesses and startups the chance to grow and gain visibility. Membership includes:

  • Complimentary listing in the business directory
  • Invitations to monthly business networking events
  • Access to business development resources
  • Discounts on workshops and training programs

Perfect for businesses just getting started and looking to make a mark.

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership is tailored for established businesses looking for enhanced support and services. Benefits include:

  • Priority listing in the business directory
  • Access to exclusive networking events and forums
  • 20% discount on chamber-hosted events and sponsorships
  • Business promotion on our website and social media platforms

Ideal for businesses looking to expand their reach and strengthen their local presence.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold Membership is our premium level, designed for industry leaders and large organizations. Membership offers:

  • Top-tier listing in the business directory with a feature spotlight
  • Exclusive access to high-level networking events and private roundtable discussions
  • 50% discount on chamber-hosted events, sponsorships, and workshops
  • Customized business promotion plans through our digital and print channels

This membership level is perfect for businesses that want to take a leadership role in the local commerce scene.
