Curitiba's Economy

Curitiba has a diversified economy, with a strong presence of the automotive, information technology, and food industries. The city is also a major financial center in Brazil, with several banks and financial institutions headquartered in the city.

Curitiba-PR

Curitiba is the capital of the Brazilian state of Paraná. It is known for its lush parks, such as the Tanguá Park, the Botanical Garden, and the Barigüi Park. The city has a population of 1,948,626 inhabitants, according to the 2020 IBGE estimate, making it the eighth most populous city in Brazil.

Curitiba's Business Chamber

The Unity Commerce Alliance is a business chamber that aims to promote unity and cooperation among businesses in the Curitiba. The chamber provides networking opportunities, business resources, and advocacy for its members.
